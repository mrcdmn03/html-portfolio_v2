Summary

I graduated from Hacettepe University with a degree in Mining & Mineral Engineering and from Anadolu University with a degree in Business Administration & Management. Additionally, I earned a Master of Science (M.S.) in Occupational Health and Safety from Ömer Halis University (2019 - 2020). My core competencies include mine planning (both long-term and short-term plans, pit optimization, and open-pit mine design) and advanced reporting (creating sophisticated dashboards using Excel and Power BI). My technical proficiency encompasses a range of industry-relevant applications such as Minesight, Geovia Surpac, Micromine, Ventsim, AutoCAD, NetCAD, the Microsoft Office Suite (Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Power BI), Civil 3D, and SQL.
